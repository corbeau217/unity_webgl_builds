< PIPES >

Pipes 0.1.0b

Project details

pipe game where you place pipes to connect the ends

How to play

wasd to move around on the flat plane

q and e for down or up a level when the level has the space

use the bull dozer to delete pipes

use mouse to click menu buttons

use the wrench tool to verify your pipe connection

Notes

very early development, and mostly shelved for now

only has 4 levels at the moment and repeats the 4th level for the other 6

uses some really clunky methods for determining input due to lack of experience with unity's input system

last work was on trying to improve the level design/editing to have obstacles to manoveur around

this caused a whole host of extra stress on how to save and load, meaning that the level loading now has bugs so may not let you click next level
